Fearless Ava 


#####FEATURES#####

- Objekte aufsammeln/abgeben 
- Quests annehmen/abgeben (Quests werden automatisch angenommen und abgegeben)


#####STEUERUNG#####

- A, D - Interaktion mit Objekten (Objekte aufsammeln, Quests annehmen) - F  

- Mit dem Dialog fortfahren – Leertaste („continue“) 

- Sprung - Leertaste gedrückt halten

- Pogo-Jump - Leertaste + S + Linksklick (über einer Jumpshell) 


#####STORY#####

Ava the adventurer receives a letter from her adventure Guild:

“Dear Ava, we ask of you to slay the Dragon in the Silverwood Forest. This task needs to be completed in the next 5 days, since it will attack the nearby town if not slain in time. We are unsure of its location, but there is a small community living in the forest, perhaps they know more about the Dragons whereabouts.”

After that Ava has made her way over to the forest as soon as possible. It took her four days until she arrived there, leaving her with just a day to find and kill the dragon. As she arrived in a familiar area, she was greeted by her husband who was surprised but happy to see her. When he had asked why she was suddenly back, a shower of guild overcame Ava not wanting to admit that she didn’t come to visit her family but to slay a dragon. So, she kept her Mission a secret, lying to her husband that she came back to see her family. As a welcome back the Husband suggested a Grill-party, which still needs a lot of preparation, since it was so sudden. Ava now has to help make the Grill party work, while at the same time having to find the Dragons-Layer and kill it, while keeping it a secret from her family.


#####WALKTHROUGH - REIHENFOLGE DER QUESTS#####

Quest 1: Die Aufgabe besteht darin, die Beeren, Gewürze und Sirup zu besorgen. 
Quest 2: Die Aufgabe besteht darin, die Blumen zu besorgen. 
Quest 3: Die Aufgabe besteht darin, Steine und Feuerholz zu besorgen.
Quest 4: Die Aufgabe besteht darin, Glühwürmchen, Sirup, Lianen zu besorgen.
Quest 5: Die Aufgabe besteht darin, Fleisch von Wolf/Schildkröte zu besorgen.

Nach dem Abschluss der letzten Quest soll der Spieler den Drachen töten.
